Not for everyone

This work is for people willing to look at the pattern, not just the partner.

If you only want confirmation that the other person is the problem, this will probably frustrate you. The work asks what happens between people and what each person does when connection feels risky.

If you want clearer language, direct accountability, and practical next moves without shame, you are in the right place.

Coaching and education are not therapy, crisis support, or a substitute for medical or mental-health care. If there is abuse, coercion, violence, or immediate danger, prioritize safety and professional/crisis support first.

What makes this different from generic relationship advice

01It starts with the repeated loop.

Not just the latest argument, but the pattern that keeps taking over.

02It treats protection with respect.

The goal is not shame. It is understanding what the reaction protects and what it now costs.

03It turns insight into a next move.

The work is not complete until you know what to practice differently under pressure.
