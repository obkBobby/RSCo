What you will practice

This is not information hoarding. It is rehearsal for a different response.

  • Why communication tips often fail when protection takes over
  • How to identify your most common relationship armor
  • How to notice roles like manager, defender, peacekeeper, critic, pursuer, or withdrawer
  • How to tell the truth one layer deeper without attacking, collapsing, or disappearing
  • How to practice small, repeatable moves that build trust instead of managing anxiety

Course structure

Module 1: Name the loop

Identify the pattern that keeps taking over and the role you usually play inside it.

Module 2: Understand the armor

See how protection strategies begin and why they can start costing connection.

Module 3: Practice the different move

Choose one honest, grounded, repeatable response for pressure moments.

Before you buy

Use the free guide if you are still trying to name the problem.

If you are brand new to this work, start with Why You React That Way or Why You Keep Having the Same Fight. If the language fits and you want structure, the mini-course is the next self-paced step.
