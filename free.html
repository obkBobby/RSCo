The idea behind both guides

Armor is protection that starts costing connection.

Relationship armor is the protection strategy you use when connection feels risky. It may look like control, shutdown, overexplaining, people-pleasing, criticism, perfectionism, withdrawal, or staying easy to manage.

It usually began as protection. But over time, the same strategy that helped you survive can start costing connection, trust, honesty, and self-respect.

What happens after the download

You will get more than a PDF.

The follow-up emails should help readers understand the pattern in plain language first, then gradually teach deeper concepts like relationship armor, truth one layer deeper, different moves under pressure, and building trust through repetition.

  • Email 1: deliver the guide and name the problem clearly.
  • Email 2: explain how protection patterns start costing connection.
  • Email 3: help them identify their most common reaction or fight loop.
  • Email 4: teach one different move before pressure takes over.
  • Email 5: invite Start Here, individual coaching, group coaching, or the couples path.
