The idea behind both guides

Relationship armor is protection that starts costing connection.

Relationship armor is the protection strategy you use when connection feels risky. It may look like control, shutdown, overexplaining, people-pleasing, criticism, perfectionism, withdrawal, or staying easy to manage.

It usually began as protection. But over time, the same strategy that helped you survive can start costing connection, trust, honesty, and self-respect.
