# RobertSawyer.co Funnel Strategy

## Strategic correction

The public-facing site should not lead with internal methodology language like “relationship operating system” or “Relationship System Decoder.” Those are useful internal concepts, but cold leads need problem-language first.

Public language should sound like:

- Why do I keep shutting down, overexplaining, controlling, or people-pleasing?
- Why do we keep having the same fight?
- Why does repair never seem to last?
- Why do I become someone I do not like when I feel threatened?
- Why do I feel like the manager, employee, parent, child, prosecutor, defendant, or ghost?

Then the email/content ecosystem can teach the deeper language: armor, survival strategies, invisible systems, relational courage, family culture.

## Positioning relative to Joe Hudson / Art of Accomplishment

Joe Hudson / Art of Accomplishment owns a strong lane around emotional clarity, aliveness, high performance, alignment, transformation, connection, and group courses for ambitious people.

Robert Sawyer’s moat should be the more grounded, relational, clinically-informed implementation layer:

- Individual work: how your survival strategies affect your relationships, leadership, parenting, conflict, intimacy, and self-respect.
- Couples work: how two people accidentally build a painful pattern together and how to rebuild trust, responsibility, and family culture.
- Group work: a structured practice environment for directness, emotional honesty, relational courage, and new responses.

Simple moat statement:

> Joe Hudson helps people find clarity and aliveness. Robert Sawyer helps people stop letting old protection strategies run their relationships.

More public-facing:

> I help individuals and couples stop repeating the patterns that protect them in the moment but cost them connection, trust, and self-respect over time.

## Recommended business path

### Phase 1: Prioritize individual lead conversion

Individual coaching should be the first priority for RobertSawyer.co because:

1. One person can opt in without needing partner buy-in.
2. The pain is immediate and personal.
3. Lead magnets are easier to consume privately.
4. Email nurture can warm them toward coaching, group work, or couples work later.
5. It avoids couples-coaching hurdles: partner resistance, blame, urgency mismatch, “send this to your spouse” defensiveness, and higher trust requirements.

Primary individual problem:

> I keep reacting in ways I hate — shutting down, overexplaining, controlling, pleasing, withdrawing, or spiraling — and I do not know how to stop.

Individual offer ladder:

1. Free lead magnet
2. Email nurture
3. Low-cost workshop or mini-course
4. Group coaching / cohort
5. Private individual coaching
6. Relationship/couples pathway if relevant

### Phase 2: Build couples lead magnet as secondary homepage path

Couples lead magnet should exist, but not necessarily dominate the homepage at first. The couple path should be framed so one motivated partner can opt in without needing immediate spouse/partner agreement.

Primary couples problem:

> We keep having the same fight, and every repair attempt turns into blame, shutdown, control, or distance.

Couples offer ladder:

1. Free couples lead magnet
2. Email nurture for the motivated partner
3. “Start Here” relationship assessment / consult path
4. Course or workshop for couples
5. Paid RelationSync coaching / couples intensive / client hub

### Phase 3: Use group coaching as the scalable bridge

Group coaching is probably the best long-term monetization bridge between free content and private coaching.

Best group positioning:

> A practice space for people who are done letting old protection strategies run their relationships.

Group coaching works well for:

- Individuals who want coaching but cannot afford private work yet.
- People who need repetition and practice, not just insight.
- Audience members attracted to Art of Accomplishment-style transformation but wanting more direct relationship/practical application.
- Alumni or warm leads who need accountability.

The group should not be sold as vague “healing” or “community.” It should be sold as practice:

- Say the truth without collapsing or attacking.
- Notice your armor in real time.
- Practice boundaries, repair, self-respect, and emotional courage.
- Stop performing peace while building resentment.
- Build new responses under pressure.

## Offer architecture

### Individual coaching

Best promise:

> Stop letting old protection strategies run your relationships, choices, and self-respect.

Best fit:

- High-functioning people who look capable but internally feel reactive, resentful, anxious, armored, avoidant, controlling, or self-abandoning.
- People who consume self-development content but need direct relational implementation.

### Couples coaching / RelationSync

Best promise:

> Stop fighting the same fight and rebuild the trust, responsibility, and partnership underneath it.

Best fit:

- Couples where both people are willing to look at the pattern.
- Motivated partner starts the funnel; couples offer requires clearer readiness criteria.

### Group coaching

Best promise:

> Practice telling the truth, dropping old armor, and choosing a new response before pressure takes over.

Best fit:

- Warm audience.
- People who need practice and accountability.
- Scalable middle offer.

## Clear recommendation

1. Rework RobertSawyer.co around two lead magnets, but prioritize the individual one visually and strategically.

4. Use couples lead magnet as a strong secondary path and nurture into RelationSync.
5. Build group coaching as the bridge offer once the email list has enough warm leads.
